Frequently Asked Questions

  • Who is AIMR?

    We are a Cohort of Data Science students who practice the art of developing and analyzing big data through deep learning. AIMR webservice is a platform where researchers, developers, and scientists can test the strength and accuracy of AI models in medical research.

  • What does AIMR do?

    We collect data from reputable sources and transform the data to fit and train AI models. We analyze the models to determine the strength and accuracy of predicted outcomes. Observations are documented about how the predicted outcome is influenced by measurable factors. The predictions are for research purposes only and should not be taken as medical advice.

  • Why do we collect Age demographics?

    Age is a human factor that can be measured against medical related outcomes such as cancer survival rate. This is a factor which influences the strength and accuracy of AIMR predictions.

  • Why do we collect Income demographics ?

    Income is a economic factor that can be measured against medical related outcomes such as cancer survival rate. This is a factor which influences the strength and accuracy of AIMR predictions.
